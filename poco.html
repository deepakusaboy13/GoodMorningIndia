2. Battery: The "Kumbhakarna" Situation

Don't just look at the battery icon.

  • Open Command Prompt (search 'cmd').
  • Type: powercfg /batteryreport
  • Press Enter. It will generate a report.

Check the "Full Charge Capacity" vs "Design Capacity". If it's below 70%, ask for a discount or a new battery.
